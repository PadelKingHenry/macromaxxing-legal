Privacy Policy

MacroMaxxing - Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how MacroMaxxing collects and protects information when you use our automated TikTok content tool.

2. Information We Access

Via the TikTok API, the App accesses: video posting on behalf of the authorized account, and performance analytics including views, likes, comments, and shares.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used solely to operate core functions: publishing recipe content and displaying analytics. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

Minimal data is stored locally including post records and scheduling information. No personal data is transmitted beyond what the TikTok API requires.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke the App access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok account settings.

6. Contact

Contact us at the email registered with the TikTok developer account.
